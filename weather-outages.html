Severe Weather & Power Outage Dashboard

Use the sections below to prep early, log outages, and react quickly when conditions shift.

Work through these basics before storms arrive so power, water, livestock, and shelter needs are already covered.

  • Top off generator fuel and review safe outdoor generator placement.
  • Make sure the well pump backup plan is ready if power goes out.
  • Store extra drinking water and fill tubs or containers for household use.
  • Move livestock toward the safest available shelter or windbreak.
  • Review who will use the tornado shelter and how to get there fast.
  • Keep flashlights, batteries, radios, and chargers together in one place.
  • Protect refrigerator and freezer contents by limiting door opening.
  • Secure gates, loose tools, feed containers, and lightweight outdoor items.
  • Check medications, refrigerated supplies, and backup cooling plans.
  • Write down key utility, vet, and county emergency numbers before service is interrupted.

Log outage details, generator status, and updates so your household can track what happened and what still needs attention.
