Preparedness Cards

Each card is laid out to stay readable on paper and easy to fill in by hand.

Card 1

Severe Storm Basics

  • Move indoors early and stay away from windows.
  • Do not approach downed power lines.
  • Keep long driveways clear when severe weather is approaching.
  • Use weather alerts and a backup radio if service drops.

Card 2

Go-Bag Quick List

  • Water, snacks, medications, flashlight, charger, and first aid.
  • Warm layers, sturdy shoes, gloves, and rain gear.
  • Printed contacts, copies of IDs, and spare keys.
  • Map, gate codes, and animal notes for rural travel.

Card 5

Quick Evacuation Checklist

  • Grab go-bags, medications, documents, and chargers.
  • Load pets or animal supplies that leave with you.
  • Shut off utilities only if directed and safe.
  • Lock up, leave a note if needed, and go early.
